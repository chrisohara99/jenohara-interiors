This Statement of Work ("SOW") describes the services to be provided by Jen O'Hara Interiors ("JOI") to Customer for decorating and design services, and when executed by the parties, will be considered the entirety of the Agreement without further action.

Key Assumptions

This SOW is developed on the basis of the following assumptions, which are material to the parties' ability to perform their obligations hereunder.

  1. Customer's requirements and project scope are accurately reflected in this SOW. If new requirements or scope changes emerge, the parties agree to negotiate in good faith an amendment (a Change Order) and amended SOW.
  2. Customer agrees to ensure the cooperation of additional vendors and partners as necessary with JOI in the performance of the Services.
  3. The project will begin no later than thirty (30) calendar days following the date of signature of this SOW.
  4. There are no implied Services or tasks; JOI will not be required to perform any Services or tasks not specifically identified in this SOW. Any Services or tasks not expressly set forth in this SOW are deemed out of scope of this SOW.

Change Orders

Changes or additions to scope outlined in this SOW will require a written Change Order signed by the parties prior to implementation of the changes. JOI and Customer will, in good faith, discuss these changes at their earliest opportunity. Customer will not be billed or accrue any additional charges for service fees unless agreed to in writing in a Change Order. In addition, JOI will not proceed with any additional services without Customer's express, written consent set forth in a Change Order.

Scope of Work / Project Description

Project Name Bar Room Renovation
Summary Complete design and decoration of the Customer's bar room, including all cabinetry, fixtures, furnishings, and finishes.

Detailed Scope of Services

Cabinetry Design & Layout: Complete layout and design of all cabinetry including finish selection, door style, and hardware selection.
Bar Sink & Appliances: Layout and placement of bar sink and all appliances. Includes faucet selection and coordination with plumber.
Countertops: Selection of countertop materials, coordination with fabricator from initial design through final installation.
Shelving: Complete shelving plan for bar storage and display.
Lighting: Bar lighting design and selection, plus additional lighting throughout the space.
Television: Placement and integration of television into the overall design.
Wall Treatments: Paint color and finish selection, moldings, and door opening design.
Flooring: Carpet selection and coordination of installation.
Window Treatments: Fabric and rod selection, all correspondence with workroom including scheduling, measuring, and installation.
Furniture: Selection of sofa, end tables, and other furnishings as appropriate for the space.
Artwork & Accessories: Selection and placement of artwork and all accessories.
